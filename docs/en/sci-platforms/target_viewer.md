The *Target Viewer* ([**targetviewer.linea.org.br**](https://targetviewer.linea.org.br)) is a service, currently under development, customised for viewing astronomical images based on a list of target objects previously defined by the user.

**Step by step:**

1. Create a new target list as a table in the user's *MyDB* space in *User Query*. For *Target Viewer* to locate the target images, the table must contain the columns `objectId`, `ra`, and `dec`.
2. Click the **"NEW CATALOG"** button on the *Target Viewer* homepage and complete the 3-step form to register the table as a target list.
3. After submitting the form, the target list will appear in the menu on the homepage.

Similarly to the *Sky Viewer*, the *Target Viewer* was originally developed for the DES survey, and its initial version remains available on the [*DES Science Server*](https://scienceserver.linea.org.br/) page, providing access to *DES* DR2 images and tabular data. The new version, being developed to comprise IDAC-Brazil's service portfolio, will be flexible and provide LSST data access for members, as well as public data from other surveys for the general community.
