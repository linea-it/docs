
The *MaNGA* portal ([**manga.linea.org.br**](https://manga.linea.org.br/)) was developed to meet the needs of members of the *Brazilian Participation Group* in the *MaNGA* survey of *Sloan Digital Sky Survey*. The system was designed to allow the team to visualise not only the reduced IFU (*Integral Field Units*) spectroscopy data cubes, but also the results of data analysis showing maps of various physical quantities derived from the spectra.

For further information on how to use the service, consult the [tutorials](https://manga.linea.org.br/tutorials) on the *MaNGA* portal website.
