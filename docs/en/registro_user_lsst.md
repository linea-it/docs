The LIneA provides a set of services and platforms that support the scientific activities of members of the **LSST (Legacy Survey of Space and Time)** collaboration. These resources include analysis environments, specialized tools, and access to restricted data.

To ensure security and proper authorization, **only users who are officially part of the LSST collaboration** may access these resources. For this reason, the **LIneA registration process** requires that the user, in one of its steps, signs in using an **active Rubin Science Platform (RSP) account** by selecting the **“LSST Members (RSP Account)”** option.

#### Important: Independent Environments

Although an RSP login is required during registration, it is essential to emphasize that:

- **LIneA and the RSP are completely independent environments**, maintained by different teams and hosted on separate infrastructures.
   
- LIneA **has no authority** to create, manage, or recover RSP accounts.

- **Being able to access the RSP does not automatically grant access to LIneA services.** The RSP login step is only one of the mandatory verification steps in our registration process.

If the user **does not have an active RSP account**, **the LIneA registration process cannot be completed**. In such cases, we recommend that the user first request or activate their RSP account.

- To create an RSP account, visit: [https://rsp.lsst.io/index.html](https://rsp.lsst.io/index.html)

---

Follow the instructions below to enable the resources offered by LIneA to LSST members.

!!! warning "Even users already registered with LIneA as a general public must perform all of the following steps."

**1.** Go to:  [https://scienceplatform.linea.org.br/lsp](https://scienceplatform.linea.org.br/lsp)

**2.** In the upper-right corner of the page, click **"Sign In"**.

**3.** Select the RSP login option by clicking the **"LSST MEMBERS (RSP ACCOUNT)"** button.  

**4.** On the CILogon page, search for and select your institution in the **"Select an Identity Provider"** field.

**5.** After this step, an **"Unauthorized Access"** message will appear. When this happens, click **"register here"**.  

**6.** Fill out the registration form displayed and accept the terms to complete the process.

Follow this procedure through the video tutorial available at: **<ins>https://youtu.be/nyRN5xVRRqo</ins>**.

If you need assistance at any point, please contact the Service Desk at **[helpdesk@linea.org.br](mailto:helpdesk@linea.org.br)**.
